Weather Guidelines Reference
Temperature Thresholds ("Feels Like"):
  • Up to 29°C: All outdoor training is permitted, including training that requires wearing bunker gear.
  • 29°C to 32°C: All outdoor training may occur, but no bunker gear shall be worn (e.g., for auto extrication or pump training). If a training exercise strictly requires bunker gear, it must be modified, moved indoors, or rescheduled to a cooler time.
  • Above 32°C: No outdoor training shall occur. Any outdoor training must be modified to be indoors or rescheduled to a cooler part of the day, such as the morning or evening.

Driver Training (Above 32°C): This is only permitted if the apparatus has functioning air conditioning. If the vehicle lacks working AC, the driver training must be rescheduled to the evening.

Extreme Cold (Wind Chill): Extreme cold weather procedures take effect when the air temperature is -15°C or colder, or when the wind chill factor is -15°C or colder. During these conditions, attendance at scheduled practical training programs may be reduced or suspended if apparatus cannot be parked indoors.
