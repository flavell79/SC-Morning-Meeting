Weather Guidelines Reference
Temperature Thresholds ("Feels Like"):
  • Up to 29°C: All outdoor training is permitted.
  • 29°C to 32°C: Outdoor training permitted, no bunker gear.
  • Above 32°C: No outdoor training shall occur.

Extreme Cold (Wind Chill): Air temperature -15°C or colder, or wind chill -15°C or colder. Reduce/suspend practical training.

Wind: Sustained wind or gusts above 50 km/h warrant re-evaluating outdoor evolutions (ladders, aerial ops, etc).
